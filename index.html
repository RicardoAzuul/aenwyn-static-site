Spellcasting

Concentration is two things: concentrating on a spell = standard action, or trying to concentrate while being distracted = free action, but requires Concentration check.

Spellcasting Feats

  • Cloaked Casting: +1 to spell DC on spell that targets foe that is denied a Dex bonus to AC: surprised, has not acted yet in combat
  • Surprise casting: when succesfully using Bluff to feint in combat, target is denied DEX bonus to AC for next melee atk you make, or next spell you cast. Must remain in melee, atk must be made/spell cast on or before your next turn. Feinting = standard action.
  • Spell Focus (Enchantment): +1 to spell DC
  • Silent Spell: silent spell is cast with no verbal components. Spells without verbal components are not affected. Spell uses up spell slot one level higher than spellâ€™s actual level.
